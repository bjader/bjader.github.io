muLab

www.mulab.co.uk (no longer registered)

I led the website development for muLab, a virtual laboratory environment for undergraduate physics experiments.

Working with the design team, my job was to construct and publish a website to have the following functionality:

  1. Showcase and advertise the developed software
  2. House an open bank of student resources available to customers

As part of working within a small team, I was also involved in both developing the software (written in Python) and working closely on the design elements of the product.

The muLab project was successfully delivered on time and won high appraisal from the client (University College London).
