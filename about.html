My name is Ben and I'm a PhD researcher at the Unversity of Oxford. I strongly believe that quantum computers can offer a huge amount to society and will begin to find commercial significance over the next 5 years. As such, my research is focused on developing applications for quantum computers and running them on the first generation of devices such as the ones built by IBM. It is vital that start developing non-trivial quantum applications now, so that they are ready to be used as soon as we achieve quantum supremacy. This is expected to be in the 5-10 year range, but could be much earlier for certain specific problems.

For my MSci thesis, I built a Markov-Chain Monte Carlo algorithm to study quantum entanglement of exotic quantum materials.
